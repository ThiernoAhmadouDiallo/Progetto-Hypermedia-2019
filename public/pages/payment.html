Shipping and Payment Information

Shipping Details

Orders are shipped in the order they are received within 24 to 48 hours (except during Holidays). Expedited shipment orders, such as Priority and Express Shipping, are elevated in priority to meet delivery time deadlines. Orders are usually processed and shipped within 2-6 business days from the time the order is placed. All orders are shipped via USPS or UPS with tracking. *International shipment time varies.
